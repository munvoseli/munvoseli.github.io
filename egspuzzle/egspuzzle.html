Fight the goo

Rules:

  • The goal of the game is to get rid of all the goo.
  • Get rid of goo by clicking on it.
  • You can only get rid of goo if the amount of eyes in a cell is less than or equal to the amount of neighboring cells that also have goo. (for a maximum of 8)

Sorry if you're colorblind. I tried to make a brightness difference, but I'm not sure if it's noticeable.
